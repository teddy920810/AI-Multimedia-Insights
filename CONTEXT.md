# AI Multimedia Insights - Project Context

## Project Purpose

This repository is not a company blog.

It is a long-term personal knowledge base and writing project focused on:

* AI Multimedia
* Video Enhancement
* Image Enhancement
* Multimedia APIs
* Agent-first SaaS
* Developer Platforms
* Media Infrastructure

The goal is to document industry observations, product lessons, technical concepts, and business insights from working in the multimedia AI industry.

Articles should prioritize:

1. Education
2. Industry insights
3. First-hand observations
4. Technical explanations

Articles should NOT read like product marketing content.

## Writing Principles

### 1. Observation First

Prefer:

"Here is an interesting pattern I noticed."

Instead of:

"Here is why our product is great."

---

### 2. Explain Industry Reality

Good topics:

* Why AI video enhancement is misunderstood
* Why some APIs succeed while others fail
* Why developers care about SLA more than model quality
* Why studio users behave differently from individual users

---

### 3. Avoid Product Promotion

Do not directly promote any company product.

Products can be mentioned only as examples:

* Topaz
* Runway
* Adobe
* ComfyUI
* Cloudinary
* HeyGen
* HitPaw

The article should remain useful even if all product names are removed.

---

### 4. Prefer First-Hand Insights

Articles should prioritize:

* Lessons learned
* Failed assumptions
* Industry observations
* User behavior patterns

Instead of generic AI-generated explanations.

## Language Workflow

All research, discussion, brainstorming, and drafting should happen in Chinese.

The author thinks and reasons in Chinese.

Only after the Chinese version is finalized should an English version be created.

The English version should NOT be a translation.

It should be rewritten for native English readers while preserving the core ideas.

Target publication platforms:

- Medium
- LinkedIn
- Developer communities

The English version should read like an original article written by an English-speaking practitioner.
